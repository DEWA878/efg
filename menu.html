Desserts

Classic Tiramisu

Indulge in the rich and creamy layers of our Classic Tiramisu. This beloved Italian dessert features coffee-soaked ladyfingers layered with a smooth mascarpone cheese mixture, dusted with cocoa powder for a delightful finish. Each bite offers a perfect balance of flavors and textures.

Chocolate Lava Cake

Satisfy your chocolate cravings with our decadent Chocolate Lava Cake. This warm, gooey cake has a molten chocolate center that oozes out when you cut into it. Served with a scoop of vanilla ice cream and a drizzle of raspberry sauce, it's a chocolate lover's dream.

New York Cheesecake

Enjoy a slice of our creamy and velvety New York Cheesecake. This classic dessert features a rich, smooth cheesecake filling on a buttery graham cracker crust. Served with your choice of fresh berries, caramel, or chocolate sauce for an added touch of sweetness.

Crème Brûlée

Delight in the elegance of our Crème Brûlée. This French classic features a rich and creamy custard base topped with a layer of caramelized sugar. The contrasting textures of the silky custard and the crisp, caramelized top create a luxurious dessert experience.

Apple Pie à la Mode

Relish the comforting flavors of our Apple Pie à la Mode. This traditional dessert features a flaky, buttery crust filled with spiced apples. Served warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream, it's the perfect blend of sweet, tart, and creamy.

Lemon Tart

Enjoy the refreshing and tangy flavors of our Lemon Tart. This dessert features a crisp, buttery tart shell filled with a smooth and zesty lemon curd. Topped with a dusting of powdered sugar and fresh berries, it's a bright and delightful treat.

Panna Cotta

Indulge in the silky smooth texture of our Panna Cotta. This creamy Italian dessert is flavored with vanilla and served with a topping of fresh berries and a drizzle of berry coulis. Light and refreshing, it's the perfect end to any meal.

Banoffee Pie

Satisfy your sweet tooth with our Banoffee Pie. This British favorite features a buttery biscuit base, a layer of rich toffee, sliced bananas, and a generous topping of whipped cream. Finished with a sprinkle of chocolate shavings, it's a delightful blend of flavors and textures.

Chocolate Mousse

Enjoy the light and airy decadence of our Chocolate Mousse. This rich and creamy dessert is made with high-quality dark chocolate, whipped to perfection for a smooth and velvety texture. Garnished with fresh berries and a mint leaf, it's an elegant treat.

Tartufo

Delight in the indulgence of our Tartufo. This Italian ice cream dessert features a core of cherry or raspberry sorbet, encased in rich chocolate gelato, and coated with a layer of chocolate and nuts. Each bite is a delightful surprise.
